Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

At EthnoFlow Journal, accessible from your website, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by EthnoFlow and how we use it.

Log Files

EthnoFlow follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected by log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. These are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

Cookies and Web Beacons

Like any other website, EthnoFlow uses "cookies". These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited. The information is used to optimize the users' experience by customizing our web page content based on visitors' browser type and/or other information.

Third-Party Privacy Policies

EthnoFlow's Privacy Policy does not apply to other advertisers or websites. Thus, we are advising you to consult the respective Privacy Policies of these third-party ad servers (such as Google AdSense) for more detailed information. It may include their practices and instructions about how to opt-out of certain options.
